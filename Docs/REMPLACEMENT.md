before use Tier3, I recommand to install alternatives**

Here are the best replacements used by most Ruvomain users:

You must install other keyboard → Heliboard, Gboard or SwiftKey – Faster and more accurate

- Camera → GCam (S24 dedicated port) – Significantly better dynamic range and night shots

- Launcher → Niagara Launcher or KISS Launcher Extremely lightweight and fast

- Dialer & Messages → Fossify Phone and Fossify Messages or other open-source.
Or Google Phone + Google Messages – Better spam protection and RCS

- Gallery → Fossify Galery or Simple Gallery – Clean and bloat-free

- File Manager→ Material Files or MiXplorer
